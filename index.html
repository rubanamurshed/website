About me

Driven by curiosity,
grounded in data.

I work at the Douglas Research Centre in Montréal with two labs — Dr. Chakravarty's CoBrA Lab (touchscreen cognitive testing in mice) and Dr. Raucher-Chêné's CRISP Lab (neuroimaging in schizophrenia and psychosis).

My background spans psychology and biomedical sciences from McGill, plus industry experience at Lightspeed Commerce and Unity Technologies. Research is my intellectual home — but I bring the rigour of science wherever I go.
